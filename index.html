Professional Bio

About Me

My name is Zihuan Wang, and I have a background in Mechanical Engineering. I am currently expanding my skills in Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics, and through my recent coursework in AIML-500: Foundations of Machine Learning, I have been exploring how machine learning techniques can be applied to real-world mechanical systems, engineering problems, and intelligent design.

With a strong interest in precision, structure, and how things work, I am passionate about combining traditional mechanical knowledge with modern AI-driven approaches. I am especially interested in using data, algorithms, and analytical thinking to better understand mechanical performance, improve efficiency, and support smarter decision-making in engineering contexts.

Outside of academics, I enjoy riding motorcycles and studying detailed mechanical designs. My curiosity about engines, machines, and fine mechanical work reflects my hands-on mindset and my appreciation for both technical craftsmanship and innovation. Moving forward, I hope to continue developing my skills at the intersection of mechanical engineering, machine learning, and data analysis.

Personal Value Proposition

I connect mechanical engineering knowledge with AI and data analytics to understand complex systems, improve performance, and support smarter engineering decisions.
